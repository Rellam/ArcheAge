New Area: Garden

Map

The garden is an area without housing zones.
The climates inside of it are Tropical, Arid and Subartic.

Location

You enter the Garden by passing through the garden gate located in the "Navel of the World".
The road towards this location is found north-west of the Hall of Warriors, where you will find a gatekeeper who invites you to travel towards the Garden.
After going through this gate you will teleport to the Gatekeeper's Hall. Upon entering for the first time you will unlock an entry for it in your teleport book.
Inside the Gatekeeper's Hall you will find 5 portals that teleport you to certain regions within the garden.

Region

The Garden is a conflict area. Which follows the following cycle

Conflict War Peace
10 minutes 50 minutes 60 minutes

Inside the Garden there are 5 camps, each connected to the Gatekeeper's Hall. You travel to these from the Gatekeeper's Hall.
The 'Queen Innis' portal is only unlocked after you complete the main story quest line up to a certain point (The start of Chapter 4).

Mechanics of the Garden

The garden is a unique place with several new and unique mechanics in the game.
Inside of the garden you will encounter hostile forces from all servers.
Each faction will be classified on their own. (Ex. Alexander-Nuia)

Three factions will be paired against each other per Garden Instance, every X amount of days these pairings will be changed.
All existing relationships between factions will not count within the Garden Instance.
Ex. "Alexander-Nuia", "Halnaak-Nuia", "Belstrom-Pirates" will be in the same Garden Instance but all 3 are hostile to each other.
Certain functions such as Guild, Family and Cash Shop will not be available within the Garden.

There are 4 Respawn bases (Nui's) that you can spawn at when you die, where you respawn is based on your location where you die.
These respawn bases are shared between all factions in the Garden, but you can quickly move between them by using the 'Resurrection Tree' in these bases.

Equipment Specialisation

Equipment specialisation is a new mechanic that further enhances weapons and armor.
Specialisation can only be applied to items above a certain grade.
It is done through the consumption of synthesis experience, the amount of experience required depends on the equipment type and specialisation level.

For weapons it will increase the amount of bonus damage you deal / amount of bonus healing you do versus certain armor types.
(Ex. Bow does more bonus damage to cloth).
For armor it increases your resistance against the weapon counterpart. (Ex. Cloth receives less bonus damage from bows.)

Castle Territory Changes

The castles get expanded with new features. This is in the form of advanced territory structures.
Each advanced structure will have different benefits, below you can find a brief overview of the new functions.

Guardian Altar


When a hero prays at the guardian altar a Caretaker NPC will spawn.
The hero can deliver a Protective Shield Activator to this caretaker and he will spawn a protective bubble around the castle's Steamy territory oasis.

  • The protective bubble will last for 6 hours and prevents entry from outside.
  • The bubble only prevents entry, ranged attacks can still go through it.
  • When you are inside of the bubble while you have the Territory bathing suit equipped, you will heal for 750 health per 5 seconds.

You can enter the bubble by talking to the Caretaker.
if you wish to leave the bubble, you can do so by talking to an estate official who is enjoying the Oasis himself.

Shield Adjustments

The extra physical or magical defence has been removed from Hiram and Crafted shields.
This has been done so shields such as Black Dragon shield and Abyssal Library shield gain more viability.
To compensate for this loss the base defence of shields has been increased.
As a result Hiram and Crafted shields will have a slightly reduced defence and Abyssal Library / Black Dragon a slightly increased.

Smuggling Contraband

Smuggling is a new way of trade running. It adds new packs to the game that can be crafted at the castle territories.
These packs are crafted at the smuggling machine in the Territory Headquarters (Place where you turn in Territory Packs near the beach).
To craft them you need a combination of processed garden vegetation and a burning log or auroria mineral water.
After a short quest line you will unlock the ability to get "Processed Garden Vegetation" through a daily quest.
The daily quest requires you to turn in 3 Territory coins.

God Powers

God powers are powers aimed to strengthen heroes of your faction. They can acquire these powers to turn the tide of battle.
These powers are earned through defeating Shadow Eaters that spawn throughout the Garden.

Shadow Eater



Shadow Eater's spawn every 2 hours after 7pm Server time until 11am the next day apart from Saturdays.
Defeating a Shadow Eater spawns a Divine Orb at a random location through the Garden.
If you don't kill the Shadow Eater it will despawn after 1 hour.

Divine Orb



These Divine Orbs can only be captured by a Hero with his Hero cape equipped.
If no one captures the Orb within 1 hour it despawns.
When a hero captures the power the whole raid is rewarded with a "Power Fragment"
The power fragment is an item that grants one of the following items when used

Divine Throne


After capturing the Divine Orb the hero has to bring it the Divine Throne, here he will learn to utilise the power within the Divine Orb and gain access to it's God Power.

God Power Effects

The power lasts for 18 hours.
The powers can not be used in instances or battlegrounds.
There is two categories of powers: Useable and Continuous powers.
Useable powers have a cooldown of 5 minutes and can be used a total of 3 times.
  • They disappear when used 3 times or after 18 hours.
Continous powers last for 18 hours.

The way to progress the quest in the instance has been changed. The rewards and difficulty have been tweaked.

Balance Changes

Skill Adjustment
  • Overall
    • Shaken
      • Movement speed slow removed
      • Added cast time increase effect (more cast time)
      • Duration increased from 3 seconds to 5 seconds
      • Distressed is unaffected by the changes.
  • Defence
    • Redoubt: Can be used without a shield
      • Basic and Life Ancestral: Duration increased from 5 to 6 seconds. 3 seconds duration without a shield.
      • Gale Ancestral: Duratoin increased from 2 to 3 seconds. 1.5 seconds duration without a shield.
    • Refreshment: Cooldown with shield decreased from 2 minutes to 1 minute. 2 minutes cooldown without a shield.
  • Vitalism
    • Mana Barrier: The shield is no longer applied to yourself.
    • Can now be targetted on all allies.
    • The shield is only applied to party / raid members.
    • Awaiting confirmation on these changes to Mana Barrier.
  • Auramancy
    • Shrug It Off: Cooldown decreased from 2 minutes to 1 minute 20 seconds.
  • Shadowplay
    • Shadowsmite: The additional damage when coming from Stealth has been decreased from 98% to 48%.
  • Malediction
    • Crashing Waves: Automatic Targetting Range lowered from 20 meters to 12 meters. (Total ability range still 20 meters)
    • Malicious Binding: Is now classified as a confinement.
  • Battlerage
    • Triple Slash: Snared targets are no longer shaken, but applies a 27% movement speed slow instead.
    • Behind Enemy Lines: Enemies hit are no longer shaken, but applies a 27% movement speed slow instead.
    • Puncture: Defence penetration has been decreased from 3000 to 1000.
      Defence penetration remains 3000 while using a two-handed weapon.
  • Swiftblade
    • Instinct:
      • Can not be removed by Swiftblade abilities.
      • Increased damage done by Swiftblade skills has been increased from 50% to 60%.
      • Duration has been decreased from 5 seconds to 3 seconds.
    • Blade Flurry:
      • Blade Flurry (Basic):
        • Added left hand weapon damage.
        • Reduces cooldown of Crescent Slice by 30% when hitting an enemy under the effect of instinct.
      • Blade Flurry (Wave):
        • Added forward dash.
        • Can not pass through enemies who have instinct applied.
        • Applies instinct.
        • Reduces cooldown of Crescent Slice by 30% when hitting an enemy under the effect of instinct.
      • Crescent Slice:
        • Additional damage to bleeding targets has been reduced from 30% to 13%.
      • Sinister Strike:
        • Additional damage to bleeding targets has been reduced from 30% to 13%.
      • Blink:
        • Cooldown has been decreased from 35 seconds to 24 seconds.
        • When hitting an enemy under the effect of Enervate, the enemy is feared.
        • Hitting targets under the effect of instinct resets the cooldown of Sinister Strike, Crescent Slice, Relentless Assault, Twin Shadow Slash.
      • Relentless Assault:
        • Additional damage to bleeding targets has been reduced from 39% to 16%.
        • If the enemy is shackled, the shackle duration is extended when hit by Relentless Assault
      • Reverberate:
        • Cooldown is reset when killing an enemy under the effects of Instinct with a copied skill.
      • Entangle:
        • Added Combo: Enemies under the effects of summon crows are stunned on hit.
      • Bladeblast:
        • Auto target range has been reduces from 14m to 5m.
      • Twin Shadow Slash:
        • Added Combo: Enemies under the effects of impale are shackled on hit.
        • If the enemy is silenced, the silence duration is extended when hit by Twin Shadow Slash
