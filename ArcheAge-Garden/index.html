Garden - Part 1

New Area: Garden

Map

The garden is an area without housing zones.
The climates inside of it are Tropical, Arid and Subartic.

Location

You enter the Garden by passing through the garden gate located in the "Navel of the World".
The road towards this location is found north-west of the Hall of Warriors, where you will find a gatekeeper who invites you to travel towards the Garden.
After going through this gate you will teleport to the Gatekeeper's Hall. Upon entering for the first time you will unlock an entry for it in your teleport book.
Inside the Gatekeeper's Hall you will find 5 portals that teleport you to certain regions within the garden.

Region

The Garden is a conflict area. Which follows the following cycle

Conflict War Peace
10 minutes 50 minutes 60 minutes

Camps

Inside the Garden there are 5 camps, each connected to the Gatekeeper's Hall. You travel to these from the Gatekeeper's Hall.
The 'Queen Innis' portal is only unlocked after you complete the main story quest line up to a certain point (The start of Chapter 4).

Daily Quest: Devotion to the Spirit



You can pick up the quest from the spirit at any of the four locations.
Scout the Garden to earn points through various activities.
The more points you collect, the higher your ranking will be.
The higher the ranking, the more rewards you gain from the quest.
This quest is a daily.

How to gain points

Author Note: The values of the point table differ between certain implementations of the patch.
Use them as a guideline, not a given fact.

Activity Points Earned
Elapsed Time (10 seconds) 5
Kill monsters During Peace: 115 | During Conflict/War: 160
Kill a hostile player 200
Gathering vegetation 30
Fishing 10
Notes

  • No points can be earned while you are in a safe zone.
  • Upon death, your score is reduced by 2500.
  • When you kill a hostile player with a higher rank than yourself, your score increases by 2500 (expect for protected players)
  • Monster kill points are awarded based on the amount of nearby party members (Up to 5 people).
  • You do not receive any points over time if your rank is above 6.
  • When you kill a boss monster, all participants receive 5000 points.
  • You can craft a buff to protected yourself from losing points when you die and it increases the amounts of points you get from monster kills by 50%.

Blessing of Spirits

A potion infused with the mysterious power of the spirit.
Use Effect

  • Grants the elemental protection buff
    • Points gained from killing monsters is increased by 50%.
    • Your points are not decreased upon death.
  • The buff lasts for 30 minutes.
  • Can only be used while the 'Devotion to the Spirit' quest is active.

Mechanics of the Garden

The garden is a unique place with several new and unique mechanics in the game.
Inside of the garden you will encounter hostile forces from all servers.
Each faction will be classified on their own. (Ex. Alexander-Nuia)

Three factions will be paired against each other per Garden Instance, every X amount of days these pairings will be changed.
All existing relationships between factions will not count within the Garden Instance.
Ex. "Alexander-Nuia", "Halnaak-Nuia", "Belstrom-Pirates" will be in the same Garden Instance but all 3 are hostile to each other.
Certain functions such as Guild, Family and Cash Shop will not be available within the Garden.

There are 4 Respawn bases (Nui's) that you can spawn at when you die, where you respawn is based on your location where you die.
These respawn bases are shared between all factions in the Garden, but you can quickly move between them by using the 'Resurrection Tree' in these bases.

New Equipment

Tier 5 Hiram: Sublime Hiram


Garden brings us another tier of hiram gear.
You can acquire this gear by awakening Tier 4 Hiram of Mythic or higher grade.
After awakening the item will remain the same grade it was before. (Mythic T4 -> Mythic T5 | Eternal T4-> Eternal T5)
As with all other awakenings, all your image items, lunagems, tempers, etc will remain intact.

Tier 5 Hiram has no new synthesis effects, it is just an increase in item level and total stats.
This puts the stats equal to Tier 1 Erenor.

Glorious Awakening Scroll

Use:
Use 50 Glorious Awakening Scrolls and 500 labor to awaken a piece of Glorious Hiram Equipment (Mythic or higher grade).

Successful awakening can cause equipment with tempering points above +20 to lose up to -2 points, but won't drop the item below +20.
Awakening Information
  • Success rate: 5%
  • Bonus Success Rate: 2%
  • Crystallisation is possible
Can be obtained from daily quest available in the Garden - Devotion to the Spirit
Can be obtained as loot from the 4 boss monsters in the Garden.
There is no way to convert existing Awakening Scrolls to this one.

Sublime Glorious Awakening Scroll

Use:
Use 1 Sublime Glorious Awakening Scroll and 500 labor to awaken a piece of Glorious Hiram Equipment (Mythic or higher grade).

The equipment is protected from losing temper points on successful awakening.

Awakening Information
  • Success rate: 20%
  • Bonus Success Rate: 10%
  • Crystallisation is not possible
Can be obtained by combining 150 Glorious Awakening Scrolls and 1 Glorious Runescribe Quill.
