Garden - Part 1

New Area: Garden

Map

The garden is an area without housing zones.
The climates inside of it are Tropical, Arid and Subartic.

Location

You enter the Garden by passing through the garden gates located in the "Navel of the World".
The road towards this location is found north-west of the Hall of Warriors, where you will find a gatekeeper who invites you to travel towards the Garden.

Region

The Garden is a conflict area. Which follows the following cycle

Conflict War Peace
10 minutes 50 minutes 60 minutes

Camps

Inside the Garden there are 5 camps, each connected to the central Garden Gate.

Autumn Garden: Birthplace of Stars

Autumn Garden: Falling Autumn

Winter Garden: Frozen Ice

Winter Garden: Snow Fields

Queen Innis

Lake between Autumn and Winter Gardens

Previous Next

Navigating within the Garden

You can not use Hereafter Worldgates to move within the Garden.
Inside the Garden merchants will sell teleportation scrolls.

Teleportation Scrolls
Item name Effect
Teleportation Scroll: Birthplace of Stars Teleports you to the Birthplace of Stars Camp
Teleportation Scroll: Falling Autumn Teleports you to the Falling Autum Camp
Teleportation Scroll: Frozen Ice Teleports you to the Frozen Ice
Teleportation Scroll: Snow Fields Teleports you to the Snow Fields Camp

Bosses

During war time, four bosses will appear throughout the Garden.
These bosses despawn when war ends.
Upon killing these bosses there is a chance to drop the following items

Item name Amount
Garden Boss Enhancer (20000 Boss Exp) 1
Noble Awakening Sroll: Rank 4 (Tier 4 -> Tier 5) 1
Evenglow Lunagem 1
Gold Crate 1
Forest Keeper Skenta
Meteorite Mutant Mgora
Frost Dragon Snake
